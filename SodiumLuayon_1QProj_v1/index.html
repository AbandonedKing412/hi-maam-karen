The Problem

The environment is facing serious challenges such as climate change, pollution, and deforestation. In the Philippines alone, over 2.7 million tons of plastic waste are generated annually. 47% of the nation's forests have already been lost due to logging, urbanization, and agricultural expansion.

Forest loss increases the risks of flooding and landslides, while polluted surroundings expose communities to disease. Still, it is not too late. By choosing sustainable lifestyles, each of us can make a lasting difference.

What is Being Done?

The Ecological Solid Waste Management Act promotes segregation and proper waste disposal. Republic Act 11038 conserves biodiversity by protecting ecologically important lands and waters.

Groups like EcoWaste Coalition, Greenpeace Philippines, and Eco Ways lead cleanups, recycling drives, tree planting, and awareness campaigns. Protecting the environment requires both government and community action.

Purpose of Our Page

This website aims to inform and inspire people to act for the environment. We also organize seminars and discussions to share knowledge, promote sustainable practices, and encourage participation.
